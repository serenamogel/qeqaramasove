Shalini Pandey is a Tamil actress who was born on 23rd September 1993, Jabalpur, MP, India. She has been working in the Tamil, Telugu and Hindi cinema and has appeared in various movies for which she gained much recognition from fans and viewers. She is mainly known for the role played in the Hindi debut movie named “Meri Nimmo”. While she has acted in a variety of different language movies. She got her education from Christ Church Girls Higher Secondary School, and later, she did B-Tech in ECE from Global Engineering College.

Career

The young actress, Shalini Pandey has established herself a great and successful actress by working in the movies. She has been working for Hindi, Tamil, and Telugu film industry and marked her identity in the cinema. She made her Hindi film debut “Meri Nimmo” for which she gained huge popularity. She also worked in the Tamil debut movie named “Nadigayar Thilagam” and appeared in Telugu debut movie named “Arjun Reddy”. After holding a special place in the cinema, she then frequently appeared in various movies and received a lot of appreciation and positive response from film critics. Shalini Pandey was so passionate about acting so she took part in various activities during her school and college days to hone her skills. Apart from acting, she is a great model and has modeled for many notable brands. However, the young actress has been so enthusiastic about her career and always wanted to join the film industry but her father wanted her to first complete her studies. And after completing her education, she joined the film career. Also, she is going to appear in the Bollywood movie named “Mahanati” in which she will be sharing the screen space alongside Ranvir Singh. For playing the classic role, she is super excited as the film is set to release in the year 2020.

Achievements

Shalini Pandey’s biggest dream was to work alongside Bollywood’s big stars and now her dream has become true as she will be working in the movie alongside Ranvir Singh.
