Casey Mongillo

Casey Mongillo is an American voice actor for the English dub of the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Mongillo was born on October 22, 1987, in Bristol, Connecticut. They became a voice actor as an adult. They joined the anime during Pokémon Journeys: The Series, where they gained two notable roles: Goh's Cinderace and Professor Cerise's assistant Chrysa.

Mongillo is non-binary and uses they/them pronouns.
