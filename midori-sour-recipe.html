Midori Sour

MIDORI SOUR is a fun and fruity cocktail made with just a few ingredients! Itâ€™s been my go-to cocktail for years and itâ€™s just perfect for girls night, St. Patrickâ€™s Day and all special occasions!

Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword cocktail, midori sour Prep Time 3 minutes minutes Total Time 3 minutes minutes Servings 1 drink Calories 221kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 2 oz MIDORI
  • 4 oz sweet and sour mix
  • 2 oz Sprite
  • 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • orange lemon, lime, maraschino cherries - for garnish

Instructions

  • Fill tumbler with ice.

  • Add midori, sweet and sour mix, lime juice and Sprite.

  • Stir and garnish with orange wedge, lemon or lime slice, or a maraschino cherry.
