Can Redgard be used over drywall?

Answer

Even though RedGard can be used on drywall, when all of their product information is taken into consideration, it is not the case that RedGard can be used on Drywall in a damp environment, as they claim. There is no industry standard that I am aware of that supports the use of a liquid membrane on drywall in a wet environment.
