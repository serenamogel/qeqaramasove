The Best Way to Turn Off a Whirlpool Self-Cleaning Oven

In order to halt the majority of oven functions, including the self-cleaning cycle and cooking, press the button or keypad area labelled “CANCEL/OFF” (or a phrase to that effect).

On most models, you must press the keypad area or button labelled “TIMER,” “TIMER SET/OFF,” “TIMER OFF,” or something similar twice in order to stop the timer.
