Rob Schneider Net Worth: Career &amp; Lifestyle

Rob Schneider, originally Robert Michael Schneider, is an American actor, comedian, and screenwriter who has a net worth of $12 million.

Schneider is a celebrated actor and comedian who rose to prominence as a writer for the late-night sketch comedy series Saturday Night Live.

The comedy star has accomplished a lot in his career, including being nominated for Primetime Emmy and winning MTV Movies Awards and Teen Choice Awards.

Born in San Francisco, California, on 31st October 1963, Schneider grew up in the nearby suburbs of Pacifica.

His father, Marvin Schneider, was Jewish, and his mother, Pilar, was a Catholic.

The secret to Schneider’s kind of Asian look is because his maternal grandmother was a Filipina who married a white American army private while stationed in the Philippines.

He jokes about his mixed ethnic background in his comedy gigs a lot.

In 1982 Rob graduated from Terra Nova High School and then enrolled in San Francisco State University.

Rob Schneider | Charity

Schneider is a charitable guy and often gets involved with non-profits to do some good deeds.

He established the Rob Schneider Music Foundation in 1996 to bring music education to the elementary school of his hometown Pacifica, California.

The foundation paid for teachers’ salaries and covered the cost of musical instruments and other necessary equipment.

Likewise, he has been engaged in numerous charitable organizations and supports various causes.

However, most of his acts of kindness are carried out through his foundation.

His foundation has assisted and encouraged music education for high school students.

The actual figure of his donations has not been revealed, but it is believed to be anywhere between $2-4 million.

Moreover, Schneider has supported organizations such as Not on Our Watch, Animal Rescue Foundation, Refugees International, Screen Guild Foundation, and Enough Project.

Rob Schneider | Movies & TV Shows, and Endorsements

Movies & TV Shows

Actor and comedian, Rob Schneider has been in the industry for quite a while. He is most famous for his comedy film roles.

According to IMDB, Rob has 93 credits as an actor, including his films and TV gigs.

He began his career in the local pubs of San Fransisco as a standup comedian.

Soon enough, he got a chance to perform in HBO’s 13th Annual Young Comedians special in 1987.

The HBO show landed him a job in SNL as a writer.

After departing from SNL in 1994, he appeared in a supporting role in a number of major productions, especially in the productions of his friend Adam Sandler.

He went on to star in films including Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo, Deuce Bigalow: European Gigolo, The Animal, The Benchwarmers, Around the World in 80 Days, and many more.

His film ‘Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo‘ grossed $92 million globally despite terrible reviews from critics.

Some of his notable TV shows include Real Rob, Hitting the Breaks, Hot in Cleveland, and Inside Amy Schumer.

Endorsements

Similarly, he was an official spokesperson for State Farm Insurance. However, it was not for long because he was removed due to his negative views on vaccinations.

Moreover, Rob was also a spokesperson for the Taiwan Tourism Bureau and Ten Ren Tea Company.

Rob Schneider | Career

Rob was hired in SNL in 1988 and eventually graduated from a writer to a featured full cast member.
