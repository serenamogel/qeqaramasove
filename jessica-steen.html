Career

Steen had her first appearance in the Canadian children’s television series The Sunrunners when she was eight years old, co-starring with her mother. Tracy Gordon, the daughter of a character played by Lindsay Wagner, was her character in the 1986 TV movie Young Again.

As of March 2019, Steen is still playing Lisa Stillman in the CBC television series Heartland, which is the longest-running one-hour scripted drama in Canadian television history.
