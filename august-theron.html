August Theron is the adopted daughter of Charlize Theron.

Adopted kids are blessed ones. They receive love, support, care from their adopted parents. Besides, if the orphan is parented by the celebrated personality then definitely he/she will also earn media recognition. Today we are writing about the same adopted celebrity kid August Theron.

August is the second adopted kid of the renowned South African/American entertainment star Charlize Theron. Charlize adopts August back in July 2015 after working with an adoption agency for 6-months. As per the report, the child is an African-American born in the USA. 

Famous Mother: Charlize Theron

The 45-years-old Charlize Theron is one of the popular actresses and producers who has worked in various successful films and TV series. The Academy Awards-winning actress Theron prominence rises after her portrayal of Aileen Wuornos in the crime drama film Monster (2003).

Similarly, Charlize's other movies and TV series include Snow White and the Huntsman, Mad Max: Fury Road, The Huntsman: Winter's War, The Addams Family, Bombshell, The Life and Death of Peter Sellers, Arrested Development, and others. 

Theron is also a social activist who started The Charlize Theron Africa Outreach Project (CTAOP), which was later renamed the UN Messenger of Peace. The motive of the organization is to support African youth in the fight against HIV/AIDS. Besides, Charlize is also a strong supporter of same-sex marriage.

Who Is August Theron's Father?

Unlike other celebrity kids, August and Theron are kept far away from the spotlight and paparazzi and also rarely features on the post updated by their mother on her social accounts.
