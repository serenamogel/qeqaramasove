DescriptionOleana is the vice president of a large business conglomerate in Galar, Macro Cosmos. She is also Rose’s secretary and has impeccable schedule management. She used to be involved in researching of Dynamax.

Oleana (Japanese: オリーヴ Olive) is a Pokémon Trainer in Pokémon Masters EX, based on the character of the same name in the core series games. She forms a sync pair with Garbodor, which is capable of Gigantamaxing. The sync pair can be obtained through a limited Poké Fair sync pair scout beginning on July 16, 2023. This pair can be upgraded to 6★ EX since it first released.

Trivia

  • During the Galar chapter of the Villain Arc, it is revealed that this Oleana came to Pasio from a time where Rose has not yet caused the Darkest Day.
  • All versions of Oleana use special voice-over lines upon entering a team that contains certain Trainers (regardless of their partner Pokémon) and victory with them. Those Trainers include Bede and Rose.

Origin

Oleana's design is based on her appearance in Pokémon Sword and Shield. Garbodor is Oleana's signature Pokémon in the core series games, where it is also capable of Gigantamaxing. Garbodor's Dex entry comes from Pokémon Ultra Sun.
