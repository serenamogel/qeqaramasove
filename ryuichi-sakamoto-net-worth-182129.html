Ryuichi Sakamoto Net Worth

Ryuichi Sakamoto is a renowned Japanese composer and political activist, born on January 17, 1952. He is best known for being the keyboardist for the popular 70's electronic band Yellow Magic Orchestra. His work has been recognized with numerous awards, including an Academy Award, a Grammy Award, and a Golden Globe for his score to the 1987 film The Last Emperor.

About

Japanese Composer and political Activist who was a keyboardist for the popular 70's electronic band Yellow Magic Orchestra. He won an Academy Award, a Grammy Award, and a Golden Globe for his score to the 1987 film The Last Emperor.

Before Fame

He received a degree in music composition from the Tokyo National University of Fine Arts and Music.

Trivia
