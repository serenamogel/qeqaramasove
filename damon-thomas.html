Who is Damon Thomas:

Damon Thomas is a famous Music Producer. He was born on December 15, 1970 and his birthplace is California. Damon is also well known as, Grammy nominated music producer who was joined by Harvey Mason, Jr. to make up the R&B/pop production duo The Underdogs.

Damon is originated from United States. He has produced tracks for major artists such as Lionel Richie, R. Kelly, Chris Brown, Jordin Sparks, Justin Timberlake and more.
