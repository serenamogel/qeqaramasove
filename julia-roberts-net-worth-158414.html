Julia Roberts Net Worth 2024

Julia Roberts is an iconic actress who was born on October 28, 1967 in Smyrna, GA. She is best known for her roles in the films Pretty Woman, Erin Brockovich, Hook, Mirror Mirror, and August: Osage County, for which she won an Oscar in 2000. Her career has spanned decades and she continues to be a beloved figure in the entertainment industry.
