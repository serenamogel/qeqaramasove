Facts about 133

In Mathematics, 133 is a product of two prime factors, that is, 7 and 19. In words, it is expressed as one hundred and thirty-three. It is a Harshad number, and when reversed, it is 331.

In Science, 133 is the atomic number of the temporary element Untritrium. Xenon 133 is an isotope of Xenon. In telephony, 133 is the police emergency telephone number in Chile and Austria.
