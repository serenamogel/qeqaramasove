Who Voices Amanda The Adventurer?

Chelsea Lecompte voiced the character of Amanda during the pilot episode of Amanda The Adventurer. However, the current voice actor for Amanda is Blair Osako-Greene. James Pratt, the creator of the horror game, lends his voice to the character Wooly in the game. In the game, Amanda is portrayed as the main antagonist and titular character. Amanda the Adventure

"Amanda the Adventure," the indie horror game. Leigh Whannell wrote the game's narrative, and Roy Lee and James Pratt oversaw production. Throughout the game, players will uncover various untold stories about the family, ultimately piecing together a puzzle. The game begins with a letter addressed to Amanda from her Aunt Kate.
