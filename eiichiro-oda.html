Biography

Eiichiro Oda was born in Kumamoto, Japan on January 1, 1975. Popular Japanese manga artist who was best known for his work on the “One Piece” series, which became the highest-selling manga series of all time, with 345 million copies sold. He was voted the fifth most popular manga artist in Japan. His series was turned into a long-running anime series featuring Caitlin Glass as a voice actor in the American dubs. He spent his childhood watching the animated series Vicky the Viking, which inspired him to draw pirates. He was heavily influenced by Akira Toriyama of Dragon Ball fame.
