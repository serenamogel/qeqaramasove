Keyara Stone Biography And Net Worth

Keyara Stone is a curvaceous social media influencer and model who is commanding fame on the Love & Hip Hop: Miami TV show.

Keyara Stone was brought into the world on May 5, 1991, in Chicago, Illinois, in the United States of America.

Keyara was shot in her early 20s when caught in the middle of a crossfire. She sustained gunshot wounds to her arm, ankle, and foot which took 6 months to recover.

Surviving these three gunshot wounds, she took to modeling as an outlet to cope with subsequent Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

As modeling often takes into serious account the height of people, it is worth knowing that Keyara stands at approximately 5 feet 8 inches.

For some time now, she has been in a relationship with rapper Gunplay who has also starred on the Tv show.
