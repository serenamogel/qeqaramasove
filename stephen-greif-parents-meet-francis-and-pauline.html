Stephen Greif Parents: Meet Francis And Pauline

Stephen Greif Parents: Meet Francis And Pauline: Stephen Greif, officially known as Stephen John Greif was an English actor born in Sawbridgeworth, Hertfordshire, England

He attended Sloane Grammar School and briefly attended the Regent Street Polytechnic before entering a variety of jobs including troubleshooting at a TV and radiogram manufacturer and as a negotiator in a boutique West End estate agency before applying for drama school.

Stephen Greif was an honors graduate from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art, where he won numerous awards including Best Actor and Most Promising Actor, and was an Associate Member and served on the audition panel.

As an actor, he was known for his roles as Travis in Blake’s 7, Harry Fenning in three series of Citizen Smith, Signor Donato in Casanova, and Commander John Shepherd in Shoot on Sight.

He also appeared in several movies including; Danton’s Death, A Woman Killed with Kindness, The Merchant of Venice, Long Days Journey Into Night, The School For Scandal, Richard II, The Front Page, and Macbeth.

Stephen Greif enjoyed golf and was a member and past President of the Stage Golfing Society. He died at the age of 78 on Monday, December 26, 2022. His family is yet to announce the final funeral rites.
